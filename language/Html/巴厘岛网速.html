Bali Sea Cable Status

This page continuously monitors the performance of Indonesia's 5,300 km undersea internet cable and reports if performance is degraded or if there's an outage. Lines show real data packets, but slowed down 50x.

Tests are performed from Digital Ocean in Singapore to Telkomsel in Denpasar, Bali every minute. Page auto updates every minute. By @levelsio.
